# Andrew Zellinger

**Staff AI Product Designer & Fractional Design Partner**

> I take products the whole distance - strategy, design, and code.

I am a principal product designer, design engineer, and product strategist focused on agentic software systems. Founders and product leaders bring me in when the work needs more than screens: first-principles product thinking, interface craft, technical architecture, and enough engineering fluency to get the thing shipped.

My current work is centered on AI-assisted product building: multi-agent orchestration, design-to-code infrastructure, local-first automation, MCP servers, voice interfaces, and operating systems for small teams that need senior judgment without adding layers of process.

## How I Work

I embed with founders and leadership teams as a fractional design partner: sometimes the entire design function early on, sometimes the senior bridge between product, design, and engineering. The useful version of my role is not "make it look better." It is seeing the whole system, naming the product bet, designing the interaction model, building enough of it to make decisions real, and leaving the team with an artifact they can keep shipping.
